About MeroUni

MeroUni is LTU's premier platform for Computer Science and AI students, providing comprehensive resources to excel in your academic journey.

Our Mission

Empowering students with accessible, high-quality learning materials

At MeroUni, our mission is to support Computer Science and AI students at Lumbini Technological University by providing free, high-quality resources such as notes, question banks, and lab solutions. We aim to foster a collaborative community where students and faculty contribute to and benefit from a shared knowledge base, ensuring academic success for all.
